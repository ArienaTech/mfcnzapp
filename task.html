User Task

Task – Voting and manage her Favourite Candidate

  • Ariena installs the MFCNZ application from the Google Play Store
  • She launches the application and select Sign-in options via Facebook, Email and Twitter. If she selects Guest Sign-in, she will not be able to add her candidates to favourite list.
  • If she selects Sign-up option, she will complete the sign-up form by inputting her e-mail and chosen password. Then she selects ‘Login’ and inputs her e-mail and password to login.
  • If she selects other Sign-in options via Facebook, Email or Twitter. She will need to complete the Sign-up form by entering her login detail and inputting password for the option she chooses.
  • She is presented with ‘Home’ screen that show the latest news about the pageant.
  • She selects ‘VOTE’ on the left side of the navigation menu where she will be presented with the candidates for the current year competition.
  • She selects ‘VOTE NOW’ for her favourite candidate in their profile, the vote will be automatically added to the system, this will be hidden from user. User will not know how many votes each candidate have.
  • Ariena can add the candidate to her favourite list.
