Potential Users of the Final App

There are two groups of users:
End users: Pageant fans, candidates
Administrator of the application: Administrator is a person in charge of controlling and monitoring the application. This person is also responsible for making any changes of this tool.

This is their story.

Miss Five Crowns New Zealand launched in 2016, founded by Pam Cummings. MFCNZ is a performance platform pageant to build personal skills and empower young women. The Five Crowns included:

  • Greater Empowerment
  • Higher Self Confidence
  • Higher Self Esteem
  • Holding Self Respect
  • Accepted by Inclusion

Miss Five Crowns New Zealand chosen charity is Youthline NZ that provide helpline and supports for young people across the country.

Company Goals

To allow user to vote for their favorite candidates
To allow user to add their candidates to their favorite list
To keep up to date with the competitions
To gain access to candidates’ profile
To learn more the pageant and their chosen charity
To expand the pageant to a bigger platform

Company Problems

User can’t vote for their favorite candidates
User can’t add their candidates to their favorites list – there may be too many candidates to keep track
User are not up to date with the latest news of the competition
User can’t view their candidates’ profile online



Potential User

Meet Ariena, located in Auckland, New Zealand. Ariena is a software developer and a finalist for Miss Five Crowns New Zealand in 2019. Ariena enters her first pageant when she was 13, since then she has followed other pageants including Miss Universe, Miss World, and Miss Grand International.

Her Goals

To be part of the judging process by voting for her favorite candidates and adding her candidates to the favorite list
To keep up to date with the competitions
To view candidates’ profile online, across different platforms

Her Frustrations

Not able to vote for her favorite candidates and manage her favorite candidates to her list
Not able to view candidates’ profile online – not knowing who is in the competitions
Not up to date with the competitions and charity events

User Tasks

Task – Voting and manage her favorite candidate

  • Ariena installs the MFCNZ application from the Google Play Store
  • She launches the application and select Sign-in options via Facebook, Email and Twitter. If she selects Guest Sign-in, she will not be able to add her candidates to favorite list.
  • If she selects Sign-up option, she will complete the sign-up form by inputting her e-mail and chosen password. Then she selects ‘Login’ and inputs her e-mail and password to login.
  • If she selects other Sign-in options via Facebook, Email or Twitter. She will need to complete the Sign-up form by entering her login detail and inputting password for the option she chooses.
  • She is presented with ‘Home’ screen that show the latest news about the pageant.
  • She selects ‘VOTE’ on the left side of the navigation menu where she will be presented with the candidates for the current year competition.
  • She selects ‘VOTE NOW’ for her favorite candidate in their profile, the vote will be automatically added to the system, this will be hidden from the user. The user will not know how many votes each candidate has.
  • Ariena can add the candidate to her favorite list.
