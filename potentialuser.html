Potential Users of the Final App

There are two groups of users:
End users: Pageant Fans, candidates
Administrator of the application: Administrator is a person in charge of controlling and monitoring the application. This person is also responsible for making any changes of this tool.

This is their story.

Miss Five Crowns New Zealand launched in 2016, founded by Pam Cummings. MFCNZ is a performance platform pageant to build personal skills and empower young women. The Five Crowns included:

  • Greater Empowerment
  • Higher Self Confidence
  • Higher Self Esteem
  • Holding Self Respect
  • Accepted by Inclusion

Miss Five Crowns New Zealand choosen charity is Youthline NZ that provide helpline and supports for young people across the country.

Company Goals

To allow user to vote for their favourite candidates
To allow user to add their candidates to their favourite list
To keep up to date with the competitions
To gain access to candidates’ profile
To learn more the pageant and their chosen charity
To expand the pageant to a bigger platform

Company Problems

User can’t vote for their favourite candidates
User can’t add their candidates to their favourite list – there may be too many candidates to keep tracks
User are not up to date with the latest news of the competition
User can’t view their candidates’ profile online



Potential User

Meet Ariena, located in Auckland, New Zealand. Ariena is a software developer and a finalist for Miss Five Crowns New Zealand in 2019. Ariena enter her first pageant when she was 13, since then she has follows other pageants including Miss Universe, Miss World, and Miss Grand International.

Her Goals

To be part of the judging process by voting for her favourite candidates and adding her candidates to the favourite list
To keep up to date with the competitions
To view candidates’ profile online, across different platforms

Her Frustrations

Not able to vote for her favourite candidates and manage her favourite candidates to her list
Not able to view candidates’ profile online – not knowing who is in the competitions
Not up to date with the competitions and charity events
